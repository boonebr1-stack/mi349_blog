About Me

Hello! My name is Brianna Boone, and I am currently a senior at Michigan State University majoring in Information Science with a minor in Information Technology. I have a strong passion for web development, design, and exploring how technology can connect people across the world.

One of the highlights of my college experience so far has been studying abroad in East Africa, where I had the opportunity to immerse myself in a new culture, learn Swahili, and share my knowledge of Python programming with students. These experiences have shaped not only my academic growth but also my personal values of curiosity, resilience, and global collaboration.

Some Fun Facts About Me:

  • I love to travel and try new foods wherever I go.
  • My dream job would be to travel and review food or hotels.
  • Outside of tech, I enjoy fitness, photography, spending time with loved ones.
